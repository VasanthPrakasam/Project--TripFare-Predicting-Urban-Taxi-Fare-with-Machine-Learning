🔍 KEY DATA INSIGHTS

📅 Data Span: 3 days of NYC taxi data with comprehensive trip information
🗽 Geography: In and around New York with 229 unique areas
🚖 Vendors: 2 vendors where VendorID 2 dominates with maximum trips
👥 Passengers: 1-6 passengers, single passengers take 60% of trips
🏷️ Rate Codes: RateCodeID 1 (standard) accounts for 90% of trips
💳 Payment: Credit card (Type 1) is the most popular payment method
🌅 Peak Hours: Early morning (2-6 AM) shows highest ride demand
🌙 Late Night: Single passengers dominate late-night trips
💰 Pricing Patterns: PM trips generate higher fares than AM trips
📍 Distance Trends: Most trips are mid-length (1-4 km) during morning hours
🏢 Hotspots: Midtown-Times Square is the top pickup/dropoff location
⚡ Rush Hours: Peak times generate highest trips and revenue
